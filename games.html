He's an avid gamer!

Have I mentioned I’m a gamer? I guess it all started with puzzle games as a young child, but I”ve always found games and puzzles of all kinds interesting, challenging, and satisfying. From Mancala tournaments in grade school to Chess matches in Highschool to Quake tournaments like the Frag 2, where I got to meet John Carmack and play with some of the most prominent players at the time. Now there’s League of Legends and all sorts of Esports content with some platforms, like Twitch, originally designed to deliver that content to fans.

It’s been a defining passion of mine my entire life, all the puzzles, problems, challenges and strategies that you have to develop to overcome. I love it all, and I feel like it’s uniquely prepared me for a career in coding. The persistence required to overcome, the adaptability to be able to pick up a new game with new concepts and rules and familiarize myself with its structure in order to achieve. Organizing events with guild mates, recruitment, debating, coming together with a solution and implementing it accurately and efficiently. Helping each other when needed, the comradery it can inspire. All of these can be applied to so many more things in life than people realize.
